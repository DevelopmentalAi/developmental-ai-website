About this website

How to submit a new paper or update an existing paper?

First, please note that papers can only be submitted / updated by their authors. All submissions / updates will be reflected in the website upon administrators' approval.

To submit a new paper to our database, please follow the steps below:

  • Step 1: Clone our Github Repository locally.
  • Step 2: Fill this Google form with relevant information about your paper.
  • Step 3: Go to this spreadsheet, copy the row corresponding to your paper.
  • Step 4: Paste that row at the bottom of file "sitedata/papers.csv".
  • Step 5: Commit changes to your cloned repository and create a pull request with title "Submitting paper..."
    • .

To update an existing paper, please follow the steps below:

  • Step 1: Clone our Github Repository locally.
  • Step 2: Fill this Google form with information for updating your paper.
  • Step 3: Go to this spreadsheet, copy the row corresponding to your paper.
  • Step 4: Replace your paper's row in file "sitedata/papers.csv" with the row you just copied.
  • Step 5: Commit changes to your cloned repository and create a pull request with title "Updating paper...".

Disclaimer

Data accuracy: As of Oct.29, 2021, all ~250 data entries were imported manually by our contributers. Please be patient with our mistakes. We encourage all authors to verify that the data corresponding to their papers are accurate by using our submission/editing tool (see instructions below). We also encourage all authors to update the venue, once an arXiv pre-print is accepted by a peer-reviewed venue.

Peer-review criteria: In this website, we include all published results, which includes both peer-reviewed papers and arXiv pre-prints. We recognize the importance and rigor of peer-review, so we have included the venue in each paper's landing page. When searching, you may filter for peer-reviewed papers only by selecting the corresponding check box.
